Cream Cheese Wontons

Description

Cream cheese wontons are crispy, golden-brown pockets filled with a rich and creamy blend of cream cheese, often paired with a hint of savory seasonings. These bite-sized treats offer a delightful crunch on the outside with a smooth, velvety filling on the inside, making them a popular appetizer or snack.

Ingredients

  • Cream cheese
  • Wonton wrappers
  • Green onions
  • Garlic powder
  • Soy sauce (optional)
  • Vegetable oil (for frying)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix softened cream cheese, green onions, and garlic powder until smooth and well combined.
  2. Place a spoonful of the cream cheese mixture in the center of each wonton wrapper.
  3. Wet the edges of the wrapper with water, then fold into a triangle or envelope shape, pressing the edges to seal.
  4. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pan or fryer over medium heat.
  5. Fry the wontons in batches for 2-3 minutes, or until golden brown and crispy.
  6. Drain on paper towels and serve warm with soy sauce for dipping, if desired. Enjoy!
