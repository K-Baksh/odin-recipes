Scalloped Potatoes

Description

Scalloped potatoes are a classic comfort dish featuring thinly sliced potatoes layered in a creamy sauce, often made with milk or cream, and baked until tender and golden. The result is a rich, flavorful casserole with a velvety texture and a lightly crispy top.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes (thinly sliced)
  • Heavy cream or milk
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Garlic (optional)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Optional: Cheese (e.g., cheddar or parmesan)

Steps

  1. Thinly slice the potatoes and set aside.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan, stir in flour, and cook briefly. Gradually whisk in cream or milk, cooking until thickened. Season with salt, pepper, and optional garlic.
  3. In a baking dish, layer sliced potatoes, pouring a bit of sauce over each layer.
  4. Sprinkle cheese between layers or on top if desired.
  5. Cover and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 45-50 minutes. Uncover for the last 10-15 minutes to brown the top.
