Mac and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese is a comforting dish made with tender pasta coated in a rich, creamy cheese sauce. Often baked for added texture, it combines the savory flavor of melted cheese with the satisfying chew of noodles, making it a classic favorite for all ages.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Flour (for roux)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Optional: Breadcrumbs (for topping)

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until al dente, then drain and set aside.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan, stir in flour, and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  3. Gradually whisk in milk, cooking until the sauce thickens.
  4. Stir in grated cheese until smooth and creamy.
  5. Mix the cheese sauce with the cooked pasta.
  6. Transfer to a baking dish, top with breadcrumbs, and bake at 375°F (190°C) until golden.
